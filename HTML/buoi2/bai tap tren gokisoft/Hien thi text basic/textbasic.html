NOUNS

A noun respresent a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.

KINDS OF NOUN

Nouns are classified as follows:

Proper: It is the name of particular object, whether person, or thing.
Common: It is the name representing a group of thing
Collective: It is the name representing a group of things.
Abstract: It is the name represent qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

A pronouns is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally usedto avoid repettion of o noun.

KINDS OF PRONOUN

Pronoun are classified as follows:

Personal pronouns: It refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.
Interrogative pronouns: Is stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
Indefinite pronouns: It is used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression preceding it in the sentence, and at the same time connect two statements.
Demonstrative pronouns: A noun or pronoun is specified and identified by it
Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
